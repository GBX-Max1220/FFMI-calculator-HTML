Reference Ranges (Natural Athletes)

Male:

  • Average: 18.0 - 22.0 kg/m²
  • Good: 22.0 - 24.0 kg/m²
  • Excellent: 24.0 - 26.0 kg/m²
  • Elite: 26.0 - 28.0 kg/m² (Extremely rare)
  • Suspicious: > 28.0 kg/m² (Likely enhanced)

Female:

  • Average: 16.0 - 19.0 kg/m²
  • Good: 19.0 - 21.0 kg/m²
  • Excellent: 21.0 - 23.0 kg/m²
  • Elite: 23.0 - 25.0 kg/m² (Extremely rare)
  • Suspicious: > 25.0 kg/m² (Likely enhanced)
Note: FFMI over 28 for males or 25 for females is practically impossible without pharmaceutical assistance. These levels are typically only seen in enhanced athletes.

FFMI (Fat-Free Mass Index) is an effective indicator for assessing muscle mass. Results are for reference only and not medical advice.
